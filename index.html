AMIP meets demanding DoD security standards

Uilizes a layered defense strategy to restrict gaining access to the system, or intercepting data.

Multiple methods of authentication

Secured authentication including CAC, MFA and FIDO. Role-based authorization enforced.

Utilizes encryption for all connections

Encryption of sensitive data inside secure zone. Disk encryption used on all servers

Hardware encryption used for additional protection

Utilizes tokens to secure data. Tokenized data becomes unreadable if exfiltrated.
